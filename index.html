Tourn, finally Launches

Use Tourn to challenge your friends or even people around the world, to become the best out there. Follow your favorite Twitch.tv streamers and view their current or planned events.

You can easily search for specfic tournaments or use your current games library to have tournaments suggested to you.

Mix it up and use the shuffle functionality, find random game playlists to challenge your friends or even random players to the most intense battle of your lives!

Simplicity is key

We wanted to bring a few sometimes very complicated tournament mechanics to the users in a manner that is simple. Every time. With the push of a button from our feed you can view results, join events, or watch live streaming where it is available.

In order to provide Twitch.tv streaming we have integrated their API into our product, allowing the sign in process to be as easy as pushing a button.
